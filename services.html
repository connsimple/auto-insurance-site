Our Insurance Services

Comprehensive coverage options for Connecticut drivers.

Auto Liability Insurance

Protects you if you’re responsible for an accident, covering damages to other people’s property and injuries.

Collision Coverage

Covers repair or replacement costs if your car is damaged in a crash, regardless of fault.

Comprehensive Coverage

Protects your vehicle from theft, vandalism, weather damage, and other non-collision events.

Uninsured Motorist Coverage

Provides protection if you’re in an accident caused by a driver without insurance.
